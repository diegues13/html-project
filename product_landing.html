Characteristics

Appearance

The first thing you experience when you get a beer is the appearance. You see the beer in the glass and take in how it looks. This is one of the reasons it is vital to use the proper glass for the beer.

Aroma

After you take in the appearance of the beer, aroma would be the next characteristic you should notice. Hold your once close to the surface of the beer, ideally inside the lip of a snifter or tulip glass. Take a deep breath in and notice all the aromas in the beer.

Flavor and Mouthfeel

After smelling the beer and taking in the aroma, tasting the beer is the next logical step. A good, full drink is taken, swished in the mouth, and swallowed. Note the flavors and feeling the beer gave.
